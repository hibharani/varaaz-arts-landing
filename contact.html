Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our courses and workshops

Do I need any prior art experience?

No prior experience is needed! Our courses are designed for all skill levels. We guide you step-by-step through the process, focusing on healing and meditation rather than artistic perfection.

What materials do I need for the workshops?

For online workshops, you'll need basic supplies like paper, pencils, a compass, and colored pencils or markers. We'll send you a detailed supply list upon enrollment. For in-person workshops, all materials are provided.

Are the workshops live or recorded?

Most workshops are live with interactive sessions where you can ask questions and receive personalized feedback. Recorded versions are available for some courses, and all live sessions are recorded for future reference.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your course or workshop for any reason, simply contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

How can sacred geometry help with stress and anxiety?

The repetitive, meditative process of creating sacred geometry patterns naturally calms the mind, similar to meditation. It engages both hemispheres of the brain, promoting mindfulness and reducing stress hormones. Many students report improved sleep and reduced anxiety within just a few sessions.
